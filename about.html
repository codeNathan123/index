About ulk gisenyi University

Founded in 1950, Global University has been a beacon of knowledge, innovation, and leadership for over 70 years.

Our History

gisenyi University was established with a vision to create a world-class institution that would bridge the gap between academia and industry. What began as a small college with just 200 students has now grown into a prestigious university with over 20,000 students from across the globe.

Throughout our history, we've remained committed to our founding principles of academic rigor, diversity, and community engagement. Our alumni network includes Nobel laureates, CEOs, and leaders in various fields who are making a difference worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower students through transformative education, foster innovation through research, and serve society through knowledge and leadership.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that shapes the future through excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Diversity, Innovation, and Social Responsibility form the core of everything we do at Global University.
